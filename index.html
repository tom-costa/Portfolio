About Me

Hi, I’m Tom Costa - a software developer based in London, UK and I love using technology as a tool to bring ideas into the world.

My background consists of 6+ years working in Ad Tech, where I had the opportunity to work on highly cross-functional roles, helping global clients successfully build innovative dynamic digital ad experiences.

This technical and creative environment gave me exposure to many different web technologies, all while allowing me to develop crucial technical, soft and business skills.

Due to my lifelong passion for technology and day-to-day interaction with technical and development teams, I decided to turn my passion into a career and left my role to focus on learning to code full-time. I have recently completed a 3 month Software Development Bootcamp which has taken my skills to a professional level.

In my spare time I like to work on side projects that involve learning and playing with new technologies. Before deciding to fully dive into coding, I had built some websites using Wordpress and Shopify.

My Projects

Destination Checker

A search tool I created to help check potential destinations I want to travel to.
I leveraged the below API's to display results from a search:

  • Open Weather Map - To Fetch the Weather - https://openweathermap.org/
  • YouTube - To show travel guide videos of the destination.
  • Open Cage Data - Geolocation (to convert your coordinates (lat & lon)) to get the user's city - https://opencagedata.com/
  • Unsplash - To dynamically pull a background image based on the city searched - https://source.unsplash.com/
  • Wikipedia - To bring up articles related to your destination).
